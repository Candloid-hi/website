Pharma Consultancy

Health Icon succeeded in putting together the talents of its resident and contract-bound SMEs to partner with Systech Solutions Inc., USA, and other international stakeholders to provide solutions in digital pharmaceutical and healthcare systems. Health Icon spearheaded studying the localization of the digital transformation solutions that would significantly help at the local, national, and regional levels.

A Taste of What We Do

Regional Regulatory Assistance

Health Icon helped many international suppliers register their products under the local food and drug administrations. Health Icon was able to navigate through the rigorous intricacies of the different administrations across the region.

Digital Transformation

Health Icon provided consultancy for the Jordan Food and Drug Administration on digitally transforming the conventional way of doing the "Track and Trace" of pharma products into a highly-digitized modern process empowered by the state-of-the-art products and the international experiences of Systech–USA.

Digital Enablement

Health Icon provided a private comprehensive investigative study on the effect of unifying local and chain healthcare facilities under a single state-of-the-art shared SaaS ERP providing significant managerial and economical benefits. It also set the path for the best practice for newly-suggested ERP processes. Health Icon partnered with Micro Merchant Systems–USA and Odoo–Belgium to fine-tune the delivery of the best possible solution.
