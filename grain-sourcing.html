Grain Sourcing

Health Icon keeps exploring the ability to link major grain growers and exporters from one side to the places where grains are highly in demand. Health Icon has partnered with governmental and major private sector stakeholders in this feat and is willing to expand its vast network of connections even further.

How it Works

Providing high-quality grains usually follows through the journey presented hereby:

1

Locating a Stakeholder in Demand

The Middle East is a huge market for grains. With the ongoing changes in climate, it is becoming more challenging to secure the local market needs through the local sources of grains only. Import becomes indispensable. Middle Eastern stakeholders, especially the governmental ones, are regular customers with trusted credit records and a steady pace of demand consistently pointing upwards.
2

Locating a Stakeholder in Supply

Canadian grain growers are well-reputed for their high-quality production and their ability to fulfil sizable requests. Though, due to the logistical, geopolitical, and regulatory complications, they always find it challenging to embark on the Middle Eastern market at full capacity. Here is where Health Icon can help.
3

Tying Things Together

Health Icon will be the catalyst to coordinate between the stakeholders and ensure that all the logistics of: provision, shipping, quality, credit, legal, and regulatory matters are in place. Health Icon is well-aware that such type of business shall be practiced only through internationally accepted and well-proved best practices leaving no room for amateurship or fraud.
4

Keeping Up with Future Perspectives

Considering the habitual nature of this kind of demand and supply, Health Icon keeps a track record of the behaviour of the stakeholders and takes it seriously. Keeping the record clean and flow consistent is important.
